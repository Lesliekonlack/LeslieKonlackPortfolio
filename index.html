A Pringles Ad for Children - Through My Lens

CeraV: Transforming Waste into Wonders

This 13-second animation highlights how CeraV repurposes unwanted garments into innovative materials, like construction materials. It follows a young boy whose old T-shirt is transformed into a house he enters with joy. In the end, he sends out the CeraV truck to continue spreading positive change worldwide.
